Instructions:

  1. In each area, the first part asks for your general perspective, while the second part focuses on your experience with one particular supervisee. Choose a supervisee you have worked with or are currently working with, and keep this supervisee in mind when answering all the questions in the second part.
  2. You do not need to write in full sentences or complete paragraphs. Feel free to jot down keywords, short phrases, or anything else that feels natural and authentic to you. This is your space for self-reflection, and you do not need to worry about grammar or structure.
  3. You do not need to answer all the questions at once. Your responses will be automatically saved in your browser. You can pause and return to the activity at any time on the same device and browser account.
